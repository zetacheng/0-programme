## Status vocabulary

- `PROPOSED`
- `UNVERIFIED`
- `ACCEPTED`
- `SUSPENDED`
- `FAILED`
- `SUPERSEDED`

A downstream paper may not treat an upstream result as accepted unless the corresponding upstream gate is closed and accepted.
